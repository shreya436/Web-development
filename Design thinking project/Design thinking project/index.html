Shloka on Charity by Lord Krishna

दानं शुद्धं यथा स्मृतम्।
यत्र यत्र महात्मा कर्मशुद्धं सम्पद्यते।

Translation: Charity should be given with a pure heart, without expectation of any return. Such acts are the purest form of giving.
